# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in AgentWatch respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Help keep discussions useful and actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, open a private maintainer contact through repository maintainers and include relevant context.

Maintainers will review and respond as promptly as possible.
